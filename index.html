My Journey in Education

Fifteen years ago, I didn't just start a coaching centre — I started asking a bigger question: what if education in the Middle East could feel personal again? That question became Brilliant Education Centre, and today it's grown into a trusted group of institutions shaping thousands of students across Qatar.

I've never measured success by grades alone. Real success is a student who walks into an exam room without fear, who knows how to think — not just memorize — and who's ready for whatever the world throws at them next. That's the standard every centre I build is held to.

  • Proven Results, Every Year: Consistent top performers, backed by a track record that speaks for itself.
  • Mentorship That Sees You: No cookie-cutter guidance — every student gets a path built around their strengths.
  • Curriculum That Evolves: We don't teach the way it's always been done — we teach the way it needs to be done now.

Core Values

ETHICAL LEADERSHIP

Every decision I make is judged by one question: is this right, not just profitable? Integrity isn't a policy here — it's the foundation everything else is built on.

PURPOSE BEFORE PROFIT

I build institutions to solve real problems for real students, not to chase numbers. When the purpose is right, the growth follows.

BUILT TO LAST

I'm not interested in quick wins. Every system, every culture, every centre I create is designed to outlive me — a legacy of excellence, not just a business.

A LIFE IN BALANCE

Success that costs you your family, your health, or your peace isn't success. I hold family, work, community, and faith in the same hand — and teach others to do the same.

GROWTH IS A TEAM SPORT

The best educators I know didn't get there alone. I've built a circle of leaders and institutions who share ideas openly, because we rise faster together than apart.

ALWAYS THE STUDENT

I ask my students to stay curious — so I hold myself to the same standard. Fifteen years in, I'm still learning, still adapting, still growing.
